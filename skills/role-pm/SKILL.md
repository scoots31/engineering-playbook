# Project Manager lens

## Inputs

- Problem statement and success metrics.
- Fixed deadlines or hard constraints (if any).
- Stakeholders and communication needs (if any).

## Output format

1. **Scope** — In scope / out of scope for this slice.
2. **Sequencing** — Ordered milestones with dependencies.
3. **Risks** — Schedule, technical, dependency, scope creep.
4. **Comms** — Short stakeholder update draft (optional).
5. **Next actions** — Who does what next (solo: single owner).

## Checklist

- [ ] Slice is small enough to ship and validate
- [ ] Dependencies explicit (design, API, data, legal, etc.)
- [ ] Success criteria measurable or demonstrable
